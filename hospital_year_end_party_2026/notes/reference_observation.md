# Reference Observation — Hospital Year-End Party 2026

Created: 2026-06-13. Register chosen: **modern-Japanese / seasonal-elegant (和モダン)** —
deliberately NOT one of the vault's two recurring poles (riso-editorial / primary-pop).

## Design language decomposed (honest: medium fidelity — no live screenshot captured)
Drew on the *known* language of contemporary 和モダン invitation/poster design rather than
a fresh live capture (this is the §6 weakness flagged in the validation summary — noted, not
hidden). Borrowed principles, not any specific work:

- **Borrow:** deep sumi/lacquer ground; *ma* (negative space) as the primary material;
  vertical mincho set against a horizontal Latin serif; one ornamental motif as a calm
  centerpiece; a single vermilion seal as the only saturated accent; matte (not shiny) gold
  hairlines; washi paper tooth.
- **Don't borrow:** dense wagara pattern fields, gradient gold, photographic kaiseki/onsen
  imagery, festival-banner clutter, New-Year (賀正/門松) iconography — all of which would tip
  into the "tourism poster" / "cheap seasonal ad" looks the brief forbids.

## Avoid-list (house-tics banned this build)
halftone dots · print misregistration · cream/bone paper · red+blue primary pop ·
condensed-bold display · comic bursts/speech bubbles · "SIGNAL/fragments/experiments" motif.

## Aim-list
quiet > loud · depth (dark) over brightness · one motif, one seal · bilingual but uncrowded ·
generous margins · refined matte gold · instantly readable facts · warm, not cold.

## Per-element-treatment check (the recurring vault weakness)
Same-kind elements were varied on purpose: the snow-ring (scalloped double line) ≠ the snow
crystals (6-fold strokes) ≠ the seigaiha hem (nested arcs) ≠ the seal (filled stamp); info
rows share a grid but labels are gold small-caps + tiny JP while values switch font per script.
