Not a funnel

The page is built as a navigable object. The first screen borrows the spatial constellation of the lead reference, but swaps mascot planets for source material, labels, and scratched teaching hardware.

  • Central object replaces centered hero copy.
  • Destinations orbit as fragments, not cards.
  • Explanatory text stays small and operational.

Material first

The Arecibo SVG, local fonts, disc plate, and pulsar sheet were chosen before layout. Their ratios and textures determine the composition instead of decorating it after the fact.

  • Diagram: tall specimen column.
  • Disc: chromatic counterweight.
  • Sheet: low-tech knowledge map.

Bleed treatment: the diagram is allowed to escape its frame, so repeated objects do not share the same polite border.

Do not center the lesson

This build keeps the same visual language from top to bottom: external diagram, authored artifacts, dither, scanlines, broken frames, and machine labels. The result is closer to a strange educational kiosk than a promotional landing page.
